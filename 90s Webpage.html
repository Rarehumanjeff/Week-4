The Music Scene:
Perhaps the biggest change music over the decade was in Grunge: the sound of flannel shirts, existential angst, and guitars that sound like they're being played through a blender. Hailing from Seattle, the land of perpetual drizzle, it's a genre that perfectly captures the feeling of being perpetually unimpressed with everything.
